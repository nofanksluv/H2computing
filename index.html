What is Computing?

Computing, in the context of computer science, refers to the process of using computer systems to solve problems. This involves the design and use of algorithms, data structures, and computer systems to process, manage, and visualize information. It's a broad field that includes various sub-disciplines like software engineering, data science, artificial intelligence, and more.
